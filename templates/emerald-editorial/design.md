# Emerald Editorial — Design System

## Visual thesis

This template adds business-editorial authority to the shared house style. Use it when the story should feel premium, sharp, and unmistakably high-trust.

## Emphasis

- deep emerald, navy, and paper tones
- masthead-like framing and stronger headline contrast
- polished enterprise proof states, recommendation panels, and metric strips
- motion remains crisp and clean, not flashy
