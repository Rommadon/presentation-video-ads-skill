# Example brief: About Us Presentation

Use `presentation-feature-video-ads` to turn the content on `/about` into an About Us Presentation.

## Input

- source page: `/about`
- goal: cinematic video ads presentation
- style: same house feel as the bundled PresentationFeature style guide
- constraints: preserve the player shell, preserve the premium motion, keep the deck content-sized, prefer more shorter scenes when the page has multiple ideas, and use the smallest deck that fully expresses the page without packing too much into one scene

- simulation guidance: use split copy-left / product-right scenes, modern product mockups, counters, and short staged reveals when the page has strong product proof

- motion guidance: keep the interface motion crisp, tactile, subtle, and detail-driven — more `emil-design-eng` than flashy template motion

## What success looks like

- it immediately feels like the bundled PresentationFeature house style, not a generic slide deck
- it uses a sharp hook, reframing, short feature beats, and a premium CTA
- it feels UI-first, with one focus per scene and visible product states
- it lets the strongest features play out over a short mini-sequence instead of one dense slide
- the content is compact, high-signal, and presentation-first
- the deck feels like a product promo made by a strong motion/UI team
