# Example brief: Pricing / Package Presentation

Use `presentation-feature-video-ads` to turn pricing or package page content into a content-sized presentation.

## Input

- source page: pricing or package page
- goal: cinematic video ads presentation
- style: same house feel as the bundled PresentationFeature style guide
- constraints: preserve the player shell, preserve the premium motion, prefer more shorter scenes when the pricing story has multiple tiers or proofs, and use the smallest deck that fully expresses the story without making scenes dense

- simulation guidance: show tiers, comparisons, and pricing proof as UI states, cards, or counters; use split scenes when a tier comparison needs more breathing room

- motion guidance: keep the interface motion crisp, tactile, subtle, and detail-driven — more `emil-design-eng` than flashy template motion

## What success looks like

- the deck feels like a high-trust commercial story, not a spreadsheet
- it explains the logic behind pricing, not just the numbers
- it shows tiers, comparisons, and CTAs as product states or UI surfaces, not text blocks
- it feels UI-first, with one focus per scene
- the strongest comparison moment is allowed to play across more than one scene
- it stays compact and content-sized instead of forcing a fixed slide count
